Since its inception, FIRST ACADEMY has dedicated its efforts to preparing young players and turning them into the stars of tomorrow’s football. We rely on comprehensive modern training curricula, under the supervision of a select group of the best coaches and experts in the field, ensuring that every player receives the necessary care and guidance to develop their skills and refine their talents.
The academy prides itself on its state-of-the-art sports facilities, equipped with the latest global technologies, providing an ideal environment for training and physical preparation. Whether in outdoor fields or indoor halls, our players find everything they need to achieve their full potential.

Our aim is to graduate players who are not only technically proficient but also possess high sportsmanship and noble values.
If you seek to join the FIRST ACADEMY family, you are joining a community of football enthusiasts committed to achieving success and excellence in every match and training session. Our vision is to prepare generations of champions capable of realizing their dreams and reaching the highest pinnacles of glory in the world of football.
